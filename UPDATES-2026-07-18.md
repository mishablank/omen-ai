## What

A new standalone page tracking five *fundamentals* theses on the AI buildout – the
financing-plumbing companion to the market-priced indexes and gauge. Framework comes from
Paul Kedrosky on Better Offline, "Why AI Has No ROI" (Jun 2026,
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wGZboZcSGDY).

The five theses (one section each, roman-numbered like the methodology page):

| # | Thesis | Metric | Source |
|---|--------|--------|--------|
| i | Hyperscaler debt saturation – bigger IG issuers than banks | TTM IG issuance vs banks; deal table | EDGAR 424B/FWP · TRACE · SIFMA |
| ii | AI-specific credit stress (not just HYG) | CRWV/ORCL/Hyperion-SPV spreads vs own 12m range, vs generic HY | TRACE · EDGAR 8-K · FRED |
| iii | Equity raises signal debt capacity running out | TTM secondary equity (GOOGL $80B ATM + BRK); S-1 watch | EDGAR 8-K/424B5/S-1 |
| iv | AI capex is eating the economy | Big-5 capex TTM; capex/OCF bars; % of GDP + growth contribution | EDGAR XBRL · BEA NIPA · FRED |
| v | >1 GW projects don't get finished | Announced vs under-construction vs energized GW; project tracker | EIA 860M · LBNL queues · Kalshi |

## Design decisions

- Full dark-almanac theme, self-contained CSS (copied from index.html patterns: ticker,
  sticky nav, kick/shead, stat tiles, panels, hbars, reveal-on-scroll).
- Gets the **full landing nav** (not the sub-page backlink style) because it's a
  first-class header destination; `AI CapEx` link marked `.here` (amber) when active.
- All data is a **curated snapshot, refreshed by hand** – clearly labeled in the hero
  snapline and in its own "Honesty" section (mirrors the methodology page's honesty row).
  Levels marked ≈ throughout. No live fetches yet; a future `update-capex-data.py` could
  feed a `capex-data.json` via the same R2 path the other feeds use.
- En dashes everywhere in copy; em dash only in `<title>`/og tags (site convention).
