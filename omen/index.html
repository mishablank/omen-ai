METHODOLOGY

How the index is built.

01

Curation

Twenty Polymarket markets — 11 Bull, 3 Crash, 6 Regulation — screened for theme relevance and tradability. Closed or resolved markets drop out of the live index automatically.

02

Pricing

Each index is 100 × Σ(wᵢ·pᵢ) / Σwᵢ over the constituents live at each timestamp, where pᵢ is the YES midpoint. Weights are equal or lifetime-volume. Deadline-adjusted basis annualizes event markets via 1−(1−p)^(365/days); structural markets (†) stay raw.

03

The gauge

Equal-weighted mean of five families, each scored 0–100 against fixed calm→stress reference ranges. Families — not raw signals — are equally weighted, so no family dominates just by having more sub-signals.

04

Honesty

The lead-lag study finds no statistically significant lead of the crash index over NVDA/SOXX (right sign, p ≈ 0.14–0.19). The bubble-burst market is partly reflexive — it resolves on the very drawdowns it prices. Treat OMEN as a market-pricing monitor, not a validated forecast.
