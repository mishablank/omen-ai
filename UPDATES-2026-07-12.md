- Added B200 to the crash signal stack: "B200 rent below $3.00 at month end"
- Added `fetchB200()` to initialization and hourly refresh

**Live result:** B200 market-implied rent = $5.52, P(below $3.00) = 0.4%

## Update 5: Thesis-Weighted Composite

**Problem:** The gauge equal-weighted 5 families. The proposed thesis weighting (China AI 0.25, GPU demand 0.20, Revenue 0.15, etc.) was not available.

**What was done:**
- Added "Thesis weight" button to the `weightSeg` toggle
- In `computeGauge()`, when `weighting==="thesis"`, families are weighted as:
  - Prediction markets: 0.25
  - Options skew: 0.15
  - Volatility: 0.10
  - Credit: 0.10
  - Equity drawdown: 0.10
  - Macro & China: 0.30
- Missing families renormalize automatically
